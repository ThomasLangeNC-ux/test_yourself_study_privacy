Test Yourself Study Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

Test Yourself Study, developed by Tom Lange, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Address: Collected via Firebase Authentication to identify and authenticate users.
  • User ID: A unique identifier (Firebase UID) linked to your account for quiz result tracking.
  • Quiz Results: Scores, question counts, categories, and dates, stored securely in Firebase Firestore to track your progress.
  • No tracking data (e.g., IDFA) or device identifiers are collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide app functionality, including user authentication and saving quiz results.
  • To enable progress tracking and display past quiz results.
  • Data is not used for marketing and is only shared with Firebase for cloud storage and processing.

3. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely in Firebase, which complies with industry-standard security protocols (e.g., ISO/SOC). We do not access or share your data except as needed to operate the app.

4. Your Rights

You may delete your account and associated data by contacting us at Thomas.LangeNC@outlook.com. We comply with applicable privacy laws, including GDPR and CCPA, where relevant.

5. Contact Us

For questions or concerns, contact Tom Lange at Thomas.LangeNC@outlook.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app. The latest version will be posted at this URL.
